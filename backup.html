Backup & Restore

Secure your data with simple import and export.

Why Backup Your Data?

Your bookmarks, personal notes, and search history are valuable parts of your learning journey. Corsivo stores all this data locally on your device for complete privacy. However, creating backups ensures you never lose your progress if your browser data gets cleared or if you switch devices.

What Gets Backed Up?

  • Bookmarks: All your saved and favorited words.
  • Personal Notes: Custom study notes and comments you've added to words.
  • Search History: Your recent word lookups and exploration history.
  • Preferences: Your selected color theme and display settings.

How to Export Your Data

  • One-Click Export: Click the "Export Data" button above to download all your bookmarks, study notes, and history as a single JSON file.
  • Safe Storage: Save the JSON file in a secure location on your computer, cloud drive, or external storage.
  • Complete Copy: The exported file contains a complete snapshot of your Corsivo data at the time of export.

How to Restore Your Data

  • Select File: Click the "Import Data" button and choose your previously exported JSON file.
  • Automatic Merge: Your data will be seamlessly imported and merged with your current Corsivo data.
  • Cross-Device Sync: Transfer your vocabulary learning progress between different browsers or devices using the same JSON file.

Note: All data remains 100% on your device—never sent to servers. Your privacy is our priority.
